Meet the Artist

Nancy was born and raised in central Mexico. She is the proud owner of two university degrees--one in psychology and the other in law. But after moving to the United States in 2016, she discovered that beauty is her true passion. She loves making women feel beautiful, and for that reason she has spent the last few years perfecting her microblading technique on clients from all over the northwest. She is a licensed tattoo artist in Oregon, and looks forward to helping you discover your most beautiful look!
